Frequently Asked Questions

Go to New Invoice from the sidebar. Fill in your client's name, email, and address. Add line items by clicking + Add Row — each row has a description, quantity, and unit price. The total updates automatically. When ready, click Save Invoice.

AI Magic Input lets you describe your invoice in plain text and have it auto-filled. For example, paste something like: "Invoice for Acme Corp, 3 hours of web design at $150/hr and 1 domain registration for $15. Due in 30 days." — and the AI will extract the client, line items, amounts, and due date automatically. Click the ✦ Magic button at the top of the New Invoice page to expand it.

All your invoices and settings are stored locally in your browser's localStorage — nothing is sent to any server. Your data stays on your device. This also means clearing your browser data will erase your invoices, so use the Export feature in Saved Invoices regularly to keep backups.

In Saved Invoices, use the Export button to download a .json backup file of all your invoices. To restore, click Import and select your backup file — it will merge with your existing invoices and skip any duplicates automatically.

Open Saved Invoices, find your invoice, and click the Edit button. This loads it back into the invoice editor with a green edit-mode indicator. Make your changes and click Update Invoice to save. You can also click Cancel to discard changes.

Click the Print button on any invoice. Your browser's print dialog will open. To save as PDF, choose "Save as PDF" as the destination (available in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari). The invoice is formatted for A4 with clean print styles — the sidebar, buttons, and UI elements are all hidden automatically.

From Saved Invoices, click Draft Email on any invoice. EliteInvoice will generate a professional email draft based on the invoice details and how overdue it is. You can copy the draft to your clipboard or open it directly in your default mail app.

Select a currency from the dropdown in the top-right of the New Invoice view — 20 currencies with country flags are available. For tax, use the Tax % field at the bottom of the invoice — enter a percentage (e.g. 10 for 10%) and the subtotal, tax, and total will update live.

The revenue dashboard at the top of Saved Invoices totals all your invoices in your chosen base currency. Live exchange rates are fetched via Frankfurter (with open.er-api.com as a fallback). Set your base currency in Settings → Base Currency and click Apply.

Go to Settings and click the Light Mode / Dark Mode toggle button in the top-right corner of the header. Your preference is saved automatically and will persist across sessions. The app also respects your system preference on first visit.

Pro Tips
01
Use AI Magic for recurring clients — paste an old email or description and the AI fills the whole form in seconds.
02
Export weekly — since data is in localStorage, a regular export habit protects you from accidental data loss.
03
Set your business profile first — your name, email, address and logo appear on every invoice. Do it once in Settings.
04
Tab through fields — all form inputs support keyboard navigation for fast invoice entry without touching the mouse.
AI Disclaimer

The AI Magic Input uses a language model to extract invoice data from your text. While it works well in most cases, AI can make mistakes — always review the auto-filled fields before saving. Verify amounts, dates, and client details to ensure your invoice is accurate.
